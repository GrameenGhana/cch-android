Get More Time Morning Routine

  1. Drink 1 cup of warm water in the morning after brushing teeth
  2. Allow 10 minutes time for yourself, avoid being distracted by family or work
    1. Sit outside in the shade, the shade of a tree if possible, in the early morning sun
    2. Drink 1 cup of energizing tea
    3. Take 10 deep breaths, relax body with each exhalation
  3. Eat a healthy breakfast before you leave home
