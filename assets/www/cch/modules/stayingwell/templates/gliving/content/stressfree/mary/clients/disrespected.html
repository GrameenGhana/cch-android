After the client visit:

  1. Go to a quiet corner and smile, even if you don't feel like it, allow the muscles in your face and head to relax
  2. Take 5 slow, deep breaths in and out and focus on your breathing
  3. Write down why you felt disrespected and what you can do differently next time
  4. Write down 1 positive thing that happened during the client visit
