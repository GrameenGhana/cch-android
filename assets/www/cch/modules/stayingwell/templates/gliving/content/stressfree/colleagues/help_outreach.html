Helping with an Outreach Clinic


Before the visit:

  1. Remind your colleague to take 1 liter of water, cashews, or groundnuts for a snack
  2. Discuss a previous example of a difficult situation with a client during an outreach clinic and figure out 2 things you can do differently, if that situation comes up again

After the visit:

  1. Talk about 2 positive and 2 negative things that happened during the visit
  2. Take 3 deep breaths together and let go of worries and the hurt
