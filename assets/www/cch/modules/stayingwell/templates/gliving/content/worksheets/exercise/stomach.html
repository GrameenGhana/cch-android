Stomach and Intestinal
Massage

  1. Find a clean place to lie down on the floor
  2. Spread a sheet or mat on the floor, if possible
  3. Lie down on your back with arms on the side and legs slightly apart
  4. Lift the left leg, wrap both hands over the left knee and pull the knee towards chest
  5. As you pull the knee, lift your head, shoulder and upper back
  6. Touch the knee with your head, if possible. Avoid any excessive strain or pressure on your neck or back.
  7. Stay in this position for 2 deep breaths in and out, if possible
  8. Release the left knee and let the left leg extend back on the floor and let your head rest back on the floor.
  9. Repeat this with the right leg
This exercise strengthens back, abdominal muscles. It also helps in digestion and relives gas
