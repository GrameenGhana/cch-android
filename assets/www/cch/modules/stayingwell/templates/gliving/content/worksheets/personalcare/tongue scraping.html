Tongue Scraping

  1. Open your mouth and stick your tongue out as much as possible without straining
  2. Using the head of your toothbrush, gently scrape your tongue from the back of the tongue to the front.
  3. Scrape the tongue in one continuous motion from back to front
  4. Do this scraping movement 3 times to remove any coating from the tongue
  5. You can prevent the gag reflex by not inserting head of the toothbrush too far in your mouth
  6. Alternatively, you can use your first 3 fingers instead of a toothbrush to scrape your tongue
  7. After scraping tongue, thoroughly rinse your mouth with water. Spit out all the water.
Dirt and waste products in the body accumulate on your tongue. By scraping the tongue, you clean your mouth and prevent waste from getting reabsorbed when you eat food
