Becoming Worry Free Morning Routine

  1. Wake up before sunrise
  2. Upon waking up, sit up on the bed, keep your eyes closed and take 10 slow, deep breaths in and out. Focus on your breathing.
  3. Splash lukewarm or room temperature water on your face after you are up
  4. Take a 15 minute walk outside in the early morning sun, if possible
  5. Eat breakfast in the morning before your leave home
