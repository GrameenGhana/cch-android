Drink Homemade Lemonade

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a clean container over an open fire
  2. Turn off the fire, allow the water to cool down
  3. Cut 1 lemon into 2 halves and squeeze 1 half in the water
  4. Add a pinch of salt and teaspoon of sugar and mix well
  5. Sip this water or pour this in a bottle and take it with you for the day
This lemonade keeps you hydrated for longer and balances your electrolytes, and is especially useful if you are traveling for home visits or outreach clinic
