Massage Body

  1. Warm 1/4 cup of coconut oil
  2. Take the oil with both hands and massage entire body
  3. Massage face, body, back and limbs
  4. Soak in the oil for 15 minutes
  5. Wash off the oil with water when you bathe
Massage helps soften, nourish the skin, and also increases strength and stamina in the body
