Eat something light, if you are feeling hungry



Since breakfast is the first meal of the day, it is important to eat foods that are easier for the body to digest. Try to eat foods that contain more liquids and vegetables, and less meats or heavy grains as they are more easily digested by the body


Morning Brunch


Eat a good brunch around 10am



  1. Increase liquid foods: stews, soups, gravy
  2. Include fish and other seafood
  3. Include vegetables such as tomatoes, pepper, onion, cassava, kontomire (leaves), cucumber
  4. Include eggs
  5. Reduce quantity of chicken, lamb and other heavy meats
  6. Reduce quantity of heavy grains or foods including rice, banku, gari, akple
By 10 am, the body is able to digest slightly heavier foods. You can eat foods that contain more grains such as rice, banku or gari, try to limit meats during this meal.
