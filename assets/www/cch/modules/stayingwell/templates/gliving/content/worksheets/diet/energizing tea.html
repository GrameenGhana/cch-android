Drink Energizing Tea

  1. Add 1/4 teaspoon of lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon of ginger to 1 1/2 cups of water
  2. Boil the water and reduce it to 1 cup (236 ml)
  3. Switch off the flame and allow the water to slightly cool down
  4. Strain the ginger and sip the water
This tea increases your digestion capacity, eliminates toxins and gives you more energy
