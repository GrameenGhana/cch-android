Squats

  1. Stand up straight and place feet apart from each other, a little wider than shoulder distance
  2. Roll the shoulders up, back and down away from the ears
  3. Extend and straighten the arm forward, open hands with palms facing down
  4. Take a deep breath in and start pushing the hips back
  5. As you continue to push your hips back, the knees begin to bend
  6. While the hips and butt stick out in the back, keep chest up and the arms extended forward
  7. Push the hips lower to the ground than the knees
  8. Stay in this position for 1 deep breath in and out
  9. Push through the heels to raise the hip up and come to a standing position
  10. This is 1 squat
  11. Repeat for 10 squats
This exercise builds strength, and calms and stabilizes your mind
