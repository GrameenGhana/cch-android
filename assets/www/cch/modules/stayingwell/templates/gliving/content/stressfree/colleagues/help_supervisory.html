Helping with a Supervisory Meeting


Before the visit:

  1. Wish your colleague good luck before the meeting
  2. Take 5 deep breaths in and breath out together with your colleague
  3. Encourage your colleague to write down 3 key things to mention at the meeting


After the visit:

  1. Talk about 2 positive and 2 negative things that happened during the visit
  2. Take 3 deep breaths together and let go of worries and the hurt
