Folded Leg Squats

  1. Find a flat surface on the floor
  2. Sit on the floor with the both legs facing forward
  3. Fold the left leg at the knee, sit on the left foot and allow the left toe to touch the floor
  4. Fold the right leg at the knee, sit on the right foot and allow the right toe to touch the floor
  5. Sit on the pit formed by the heels of both feet
  6. Have a 4 inch difference between the 2 knees
  7. Place the palm on the knees
This squat helps the body digest the food better and is ideal after a large meal
