Hair Treatment

For Regular Hair


On the days you are going to wash your hair, you can do the following:



  1. Slightly warm 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  2. Massage the coconut oil in the scalp
  3. You can use your fingers and palms to spread the oil throughout the scalp
  4. Allow the oil to soak the scalp and hair for 10 mins
  5. Wash your hair with shampoo

Coconut oil is an effective conditioner. It helps with damaged hair and rejuvenates hair growth


For Braided Hair


On the days you are going to wash your hair, you can do the following:



  1. Apply shampoo to the roots of your hair by gently rubbing it in use your fingertips
  2. Wash and rinse your hair with water
  3. Towel dry your hair to remove water and moisture
  4. Slightly warm 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  5. Massage the coconut oil in the scalp
  6. You can use your fingertips to spread the oil throughout the scalp


On the days you don't plan to wash your hair, you can do the following:



  1. Slightly warm 2 tablespoons of coconut oil
  2. Massage the coconut oil in the scalp
  3. You can use your fingertips to spread the oil throughout the scalp
