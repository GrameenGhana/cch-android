Helping with a Home Visit


Before the visit:

  1. Remind your colleague to take lemon water, cashews or groundnuts for a snack
  2. Discuss a previous example of difficult situation with client during a home visit and figure out 2 things you can do differently, if that situation comes up again
  3. Ask your colleague to call you if he or she encounters a difficult situation during a home visit

After the visit:

  1. Talk about 2 positive and 2 negative things that happened during the visit
  2. Take 3 deep breaths together and let go of worries and the hurt
