Wash Your Feet

  1. Fill up a small bucket with room temperature water
  2. Gently pour the water on both the feet
  3. Rub the heel of the left foot on top of the right foot while pouring water
  4. Similarly rub the heel of the right foot on top of the left foot while pouring water
  5. Wipe the soles of your feet with a towel
There are nerve endings on the soles of your feet that are connected to your head. By cleaning your feet, you calm the mind and prepare the body for sleep
