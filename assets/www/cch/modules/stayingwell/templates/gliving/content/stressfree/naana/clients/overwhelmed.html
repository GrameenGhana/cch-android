I am feeling Overwhelmed


After the session:

  1. Close your eyes and pay attention to the pace of your heartbeat
  2. Take deep energizing breaths
  3. Get some sun; sit under a shade, the shade of a tree if possible, for 5 minutes and feel the heat and breeze
  4. Write down 2 things that made you feel overwhelmed
  5. Write down what you need to understand or learn about these 2 things
