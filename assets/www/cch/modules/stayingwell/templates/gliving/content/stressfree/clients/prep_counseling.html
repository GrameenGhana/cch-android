Preparing for a Counseling Meeting

  1. Take a deep breath before beginning any client session
  2. Follow an 80-20 rule: Allow the client to speak 80% and you speak 20% of the time during the counseling session
  3. Speak in a slow, steady manner
  4. Pay attention to client's specific difficulties in understanding or following your advice
  5. Before beginning session, spend a few minutes connecting with client, inquire about the wellbeing of their family and children
  6. Ask your clients if they have any questions or problems
  7. Keep cup or bottle of water nearby to drink if you need
