Facial Mask

  1. Take half a pawpaw and scoop out the seeds.
  2. Take the fleshy part of the fruit and put it in a bowl
  3. Take 1 ripe banana, remove the skin and cut into thin slices and put it in the bowl with pawpaw
  4. Mash the fruit combo with a spoon or your hands till you get a nice paste
  5. Spread this paste on your face and neck and let it stay for 10 - 15 minutes
  6. Rinse of the paste with room temperature water
This mask nourishes skin, reduces wrinkles, and treats cracks on skin
