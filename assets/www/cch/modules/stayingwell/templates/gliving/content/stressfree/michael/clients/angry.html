I am feeling Angry


After the client visit:

  1. Count from 1 to 10 slowly to calm yourself
  2. Calm yourself with deep and cool breaths
  3. Drink 1 cup of room temperature water to calm the nerves
  4. Write down 1 positive thing that happened during the client visit
  5. Walk for a few minutes outside, if possible. Or face the sun and take few deep breaths in and out.
