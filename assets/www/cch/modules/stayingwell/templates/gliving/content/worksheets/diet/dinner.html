Nutritious Dinner


Eat dinner between 6pm - 8pm, if possible



  1. Increase liquid foods: stews, soups, gravy
  2. Include vegetables such as yam, tomatoes, pepper, onion, cassava, plantain, kontomire (leaves), cucumber, carrots
  3. Reduce or even restrict meats such fish, other seafood, chicken or heavy meats
  4. Reduce grains or foods including rice, banku, gari, akple
For dinner it is best to eat an easy to digest meal so that you can get a good night's sleep. Foods that contain less meat or grains are best during this meal.
