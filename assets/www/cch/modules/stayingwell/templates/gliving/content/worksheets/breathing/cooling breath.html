Cooling Breath

  1. Find a comfortable and quiet place, if possible
  2. Sit on the floor or in a chair with your spine straight
  3. Slightly open your mouth, curl your tongue and protrude it slightly past the lips
  4. If you can't curl your tongue, just open your lips slightly making a small "O" shape with your lips
  5. Inhale deeply and smoothly through the mouth
  6. Hold the breath for 3 seconds
  7. Close your mouth and exhale the air through the nose.
  8. Repeat the process again for 5-8 breaths
This breathing technique helps cools your body and mind and allows you to manage your stress better
