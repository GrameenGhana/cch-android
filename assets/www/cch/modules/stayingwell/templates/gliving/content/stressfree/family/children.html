Raising children to be kind, responsible adults

  1. Ask your children what happened during the day at school
  2. Teach your children how to manage sadness or anger. You can ask them to do the following things when they feel sad or angry:
    1. Take 10 slow, deep breaths in and out
    2. Count from 1 to 10, if they are angry
  3. Give your children 1 liter of warm water to take to school
  4. Talk to your children about specific troubles or problems they have experienced at school or home
