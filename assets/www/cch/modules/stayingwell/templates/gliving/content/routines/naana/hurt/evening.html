Letting go of Hurt Evening Routine

  1. Write a note or letter to person who has hurt you during that day or at some point in your life
    1. Write details about the incident
    2. Write how you feel about it
    3. After you are finished, tear the letter and allow the hurt to go away with it
  2. Before sleeping, wash your feet with room temperature water
  3. Lie down on your bed, close your eyes and take 10 slow deep breaths. Focus on your breathing while you do this
