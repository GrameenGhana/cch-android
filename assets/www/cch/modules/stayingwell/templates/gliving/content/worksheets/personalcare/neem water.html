Neem Water

  1. Place 6-8 neem leaves in about 2 liters of water and soak them overnight
  2. Next morning boil the water along with the neem leaves over a steady flame
  3. Switch off the flame and allow the soaked water to cool down
  4. Use this water to rinse your body and hair
Neem leaves have antibacterial, antiviral properties and prevents wrinkles and fine lines
