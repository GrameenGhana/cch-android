Nutritious Lunch


Eat lunch between 1pm - 2:30pm, if possible



  1. Increase liquid foods: stews, soups, gravy
  2. Include meats such fish, other seafood or chicken
  3. Reduce heavy meats
  4. Include healthy grains or foods including rice, banku, gari, akple
  5. Increase vegetables such as yam, tomatoes, pepper, onion, cassava, plantain, kontomire (leaves), cucumber, carrots
  6. Reduce the quantity of food you eat
    • Join both your hands together and open you palms
    • Food you eat should be able to fill the space between your palms
Body is able to digest heavy foods at lunchtime. You can eat foods that include grains or meat during lunch. By controlling the quantity of food you eat, you can avoid overburdening the digestive system.
