Afternoon Stretch

  1. Stand up straight, make sure spine is erect
  2. Take a deep breath in and out
  3. With the next breath in bend at the hips and let your upper body fold over
  4. Touch your knees, shins or toes with your fingers, whichever is possible without bending your knee. Try not to strain or jerk your body
  5. Take a deep breath in and out in this position
  6. Slowly unfold at the hips and come back to a standing position
  7. Bend again at the hips and bend backwards with the arms and head reaching back
  8. Again do not strain your neck or back
  9. Stay in this position for a breath in and out
  10. Gently unwind and come back to a standing position
This stretch relaxes back, hamstring, calf muscles, and also relieves the mind of stress and anxiety
