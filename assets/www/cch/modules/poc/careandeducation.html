Care and Education

Keep in mind:

  • Courteous and kind: treat her and (her family member) with respect and sympathy
  • Listen and ask: ask client first what she knows about pregnancy and childbirth, before telling them what she should do.
  • Health education should be individualised: provide the client appropriate counselling specific to her gestational age and specific problems identified
  • Answer questions: ask clients if they have any questions or concerns.
