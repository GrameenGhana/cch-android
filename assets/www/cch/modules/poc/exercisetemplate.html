EXERCISE

Here are some exercises you can encourage your clients to do
Pelvic Rock
Strengthens leg muscles


  1. Get down on your hand and knees
  2. Pull in your abdomen and lift your buttocks. Hold for a count of 5
  3. Gently relax your abdomen and buttocks, allowing the curve of your back to return
  4. Repeat 5-6 times.

Head and Shoulder Lift
Strengthens muscles in the abdomen


  1. Lie on your back with your knees bent, feet flat on the floor, and your arms at sides
  2. Raise your head and shoulders and tighten your abdominal muscles. Hold for count of 5. Do not hold your breath.
  3. Lie back and relax.
  4. Repeat 5-10 times.
Squat

Strengthens leg muscles

  1. Stand while holding on to something to help you keep your balance. Keep feet apart.
  2. Slowly bend your knees, keeping your back straight, and keeping your knees and feet apart.
  3. Rise slowly, keeping hold of the object for balance.
  4. Repeat 3-5 times.
Rib Cage Lift
(Strengthens leg muscles and makes it easier to breathe)

  1. Sit with your legs crossed.
  2. Curve your arm over your it, and then return to the original position
  3. Repeat 4-5 times.
