Client could have malaria or another infection

  • Give 1 dose of paracetamol
  • Encourage fluid intake
  • Refer to the nearest CHPS, health centre or hospital immediately for client to get tested for malaria and other infections
  • Call provider and arrange transport
  • Accompany client or have a family member accompany client
  • Record personal info in maternal health book. Fill out referral form
  • Follow up with client after discharge from health centre or hospital
