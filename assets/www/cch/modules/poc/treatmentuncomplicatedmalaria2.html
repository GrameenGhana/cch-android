Treatment of Uncomplicated Malaria

1st trimester

  • Oral quinine 600 mg 8 hourly for 7 days

    • OR

  • Oral quinine 10 mg/Kg body weight three times daily (up to a maximum of 600 mg daily) for three days + 300 mg of Clindamycin three times daily for 3 days

2nd or 3rd trimester

  • Oral quinine 600 mg 8 hourly for 7 days

    • OR

  • Artesunate 4 mg/Kg body weight + Amodiaquine 10 mg/kg body weight should be administered concurrently in 1 or 2 divided doses for 3 consecutive days. These drug may be taken with high energy drinks, like glucose.

    • OR

  • Artemether 20 mg + Lumefantrine 120 mg tablets with the following doses (taken with fatty meal):
    • Day 1: 4 tablets stat followed by 4 tablets 8 hours later
    • Day 2: 4 tablets twice daily
    • Day 3: 4 tablets twice daily

In addition to the above clients should be advised to:

  • Take iron supplements
  • Eat well
  • Use ITNs and other preventive measures
  • Takes analgesics as prescribed, e.g. paracetamol 1 g (2 tablets) every 6 hours, if needed.
