Counseling and Follow-up after Treatment of Uncomplicated Malaria

  • Counsel client to comply with treatment to avoid development of resistance of antimalarial drugs.

  • Educate her on the importance of taking full dose of drugs in order to clear the parasites from the blood, and thus prevent progression of disease to complications like anemia

Also educate client on:

  • Danger signs and to return for follow-up after treatment is completed or if her conditions worsens

  • Report any adverse reactions to the drugs immediately

  • Intermittent prevention treatment (IPTp) schedule with sulfa-doxine pyramethamine (SP) should be followed if it has previously been started, or should be started at next visit if not started previously.
