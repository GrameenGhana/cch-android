Point of Care Tool 

'Add POC Editor' allows you to create the text of pages in the Point of Care Tool. Pages are items that may have text, pictures, and options, but are not straightforward questions. 

You can do a lot with the POC Editor where content is concerned. To insert an image, make sure it is already in your 'images' directory, and then includ a link to it. To see how that works, click the that appears on this screen. 

POC Editor is as simple as using word. In fact, if you have a document in word, you can copy and paste most of it into the POC Editor! Remember that you have to copy single pages, and the final outline is up to you.
