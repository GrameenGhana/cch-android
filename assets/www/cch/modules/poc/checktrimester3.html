TAKE ACTION - THIRD TRIMESTER


  • Give TT injection, if indicated
  • If available, provide iron/folate supplements: 1 iron tablet (60 mg) + 1 folic acid tablet (0.5 mg) to be taken daily
  • If available, provide multivitamin supplements
  • Provide dose of SP depending on dosage and gestational age
  • Counsel clients on:
    • Care during pregancy
    • Danger signs - Reassess client's knowledge
    • Symptoms and care during labour
    • Birth preparedness
    • Educate on complications
    • Sexual activity
    • Start family planning counselling
    • Breastfeeding/breast care
    • Neonatal care, danger signs of newborn
    • When to contact a midwife in the postnatal period
    • Immunisations
    • When to return for next visit
  • Refer client to receive focused ANC from a midwife
