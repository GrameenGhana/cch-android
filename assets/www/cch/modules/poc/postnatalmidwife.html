When to consult a midwife for visits in the postnatal



Postnatal period is the period from the end of delivery to 6 weeks postpartum

  • PNC visit #1:  within 2-3 days of delivery
    • The first visit should be at a clinic if possible, or through a home visit by a TBA or CHN or midwife if necessary. 

    • Follow-up visits maybe necessary depending on findings during the first contact.

    • Client should be encouraged at any time she has problems or concerns relating to herself or her baby.
