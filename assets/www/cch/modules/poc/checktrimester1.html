TAKE ACTION
First Trimester



  • Give TT injection, if indicated
  • If available, provide iron/folate supplements: 1 iron tablet (60 mg) + 1 folic acid tablet (0.5 mg) to be taken daily
  • If available, provide multivitamin supplements
  • Counsel client on:

    Care during pregancy

    Danger signs

    Birth preparedness and complication readiness

    Substance abuse

    STI prevention and hygiene

    Nutrition

    Start FP counselling

    When to return to a health center, hospital or CHPS facility

  • Refer client to receive focused ANC from a midwife
