Inform client when to return to a health center, hospital, or CHPS facility:

Advise her that a midwife will check for a number of issues as part of focused ANC visits.  The client should ideally consult a midwife during the following times during pregnancy:

  • 3rd focused ANC visit at 32 weeks of gestation: this is the next scheduled visit
  • 4th focused ANC visit at 36 weeks of gestation

In addition client should be counseled to return to a health facility if she has:

  • As soon as she has any danger signs
  • Questions or concerns about her pregnancy or the baby
