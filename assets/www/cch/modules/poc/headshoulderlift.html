Head and Shoulder Lift

Strengthens muscles in the abdomen


  1. Lie on your back with your knees bent, feet flat on the floor, and your arms at sides
  2. Raise your head and shoulders and tighten your abdominal muscles. Hold for count of 5. Do not hold your breath.
  3. Lie back and relax.
  4. Repeat 5-10 times.
