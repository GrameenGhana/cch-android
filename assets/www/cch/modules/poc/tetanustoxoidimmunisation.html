Tetanus Toxoid Immunisation for Pregnant Women


For women who were not previously immunised

  • TT1: First ANC Visit
  • TT2: At least 4 weeks after TT1
  • TT3: Six months after TT2
  • TT4: One year after TT3
  • TT5: At least one year after TT4

For women who were previously immunised

  • Immunised during infancy: 2 doses as above
  • Immunised during childhood: 1 dose of TT/Td
  • Immunised during school age: 1 dose of TT/Td
  • Immunised during adolescence: None
