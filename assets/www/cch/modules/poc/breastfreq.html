How often should a client breastfeed?

  • Breastfeed baby on demand, both day and night, at least 8 – 12 times each day
  • Frequent feeding will help mother’s body to produce breast milk
  • Continue to feed until baby finishes the breast and comes off on his or her own. Offer the other breast and let baby decide if he or she wants more or not
  • You will know if baby is taking enough breast milk if he or she passes light-coloured urine at least 6 times a day and is gaining weight
