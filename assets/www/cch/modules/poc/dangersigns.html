Danger Signs

Educate client on the danger signs of pregnancy, and importance of seeking medical care immediately
Swelling of feet, hands, or face

Severe headache or blurred vision

Severe abdominal pain

Persistent vomiting


Fever


Pale conjunctiva, tongue, palms, nail beds

Absence of foetal movements
Jaundice
Rupture of membranes
Offensive or discoloured discharge from vagina
Bleeding from the vagina
