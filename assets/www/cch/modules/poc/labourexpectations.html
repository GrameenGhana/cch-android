Educate client on Symptoms of Labour and What to Expect 

Educate client to go to the health center when she has the following signs of labour:

  • Water breaks
  • Constant abdominal pain 
  • Back pain
  • Waist pain
  • Mucoid bloody discharge on pad

Counsel client to make every effort to deliver in a facility but in case the unexpected happens, then counsel client on what she needs to prepare for a home delivery

  • Identify a midwife, nurse or a birth attendant in the neighborhood, and another family member or friend to care for the baby immediately after birth
  • Emphasize importance of going to the nearest facility immediately after delivery with newborn baby for appropriate care
