TAKE ACTION
Uncomplicated malaria



  • Client could also have
  • Chills
  • Bodily aches
  • Rigors
  • Headache
  • Vomiting
  • Bitter taste in mouth
  • See other symptoms of malaria

  • If possible, give oral quinine
  • Give paracetamol 1g 3 times daily for 3 days
  • Encourage fluid intake
  • Counsel client on:

      ITN use and IPTp

      Malaria Treatment

      Eating foods rich in iron/folate + vitamin C

  • Follow-up in 24 hours, if no improvement refer client
